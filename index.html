The Story

Andhika &Diana

Diana and Andhika first met at university, where they pursued different majors but shared the same passion for campus organizations. Their introduction began at a campus event, and it didn't take long for them to feel they complemented each other perfectly.From lunch breaks in the cafeteria to study sessions in the library, their bond grew stronger. They shared many common interests, from a love of traveling to a passion for classic films.
When Andhika decided to continue his studies abroad, their long-
distance relationship only strengthened their bond. They committed to supporting each other, even when separated by continents.
After years of being together, filled with love and happiness, they decided to take the next step. Andhika and Diana warmly invite you to celebrate their joyful day as they vow to be together for life.
